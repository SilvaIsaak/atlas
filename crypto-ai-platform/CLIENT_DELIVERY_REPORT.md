# Crypto AI Platform - Client Delivery Report

**Date:** [Current Date]
**Version:** 1.0.0

## Overview
This report covers the MVP release of the Crypto AI Platform, a professional, navigable trading platform with AI-powered features, comprehensive market data, and risk management tools.

## Completed Features
- ✅ **Dashboard:** Executive overview with portfolio value, daily P&L, AI confidence score, risk level, active strategies, and market sentiment. Charts for performance history, asset allocation, trading activity, and risk metrics.
- ✅ **Markets Page:** Real-time price tracking for BTC/ETH/SOL/XRP, 24h change, volume, and trend indicators. Interactive price chart.
- ✅ **Trading Terminal:** Professional interface with main chart, buy/sell panel, open positions, order history, and paper trading mode.
- ✅ **Portfolio:** Portfolio overview, asset allocation, performance history, and open positions.
- ✅ **AI Engine:** Interactive dashboard for AI agents (Supervisor, Market, Risk, Strategy, Portfolio) with status, last decision, confidence, and reasoning.
- ✅ **Risk Monitor:** Risk score, exposure, drawdown, volatility, and active alert system.
- ✅ **Backtesting:** Parameter configurator (asset, strategy, period, capital) and results visualization (return, Sharpe, drawdown, win rate).
- ✅ **Landing Page:** Marketing page with platform overview and features (AI, Trading, Risk, Analytics).
- ✅ **Mock Data Layer:** All pages use comprehensive mock data for seamless demo.
- ✅ **UI/UX:** Professional, responsive interface with loading states and toast notifications.

## Technical Stack
- Frontend: React 19, Next.js 15, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn UI, Recharts, TanStack Query, Zustand
- Backend: Preserved (Clean Architecture, DDD, CQRS, Event Driven, Multi Tenant)
